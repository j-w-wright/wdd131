Project Overview

Purpose

This document outlines the plan for the development of The J.W.Wright Art Studio website, a website created to give J.W.Wright a platform to share and sell his artwork. This may include completed artwork, as well as requesting custom watercolor paintings and sharing art resources. The website will target those who are interested in purchasing art.

Scenarios

What artwork is available for purchase?

How do I request a picture painted of my pet?

What resources does J.W.Wright use in creating his artwork?

Site Structure

Wireframes

  • Mobile Display
  • Larger Display
  • Home:
    • Hero Section with website overview
    • New paintings
    • Updates on artwork in progress
  • About Us:
    • Artist introduction
  • Artwork:
    • Paintings with title, material, size, and cost
    • Client testimonials
    • Process for purchasing the paintings
    • Process for requesting custom paintings
  • Art Resources:
    • Information, could include text, images, and videos, about the artist's experiences in creating the paintings
    • Resources that I use such as blogs, YouTube channels, etc.
  • Contact Us:
    • Contact form
    • Company address and contact details
    • Request for a custom painting form
    • Comment form

Visual Design

Color Schema

  • Primary color: #011627 Rich Black
  • Secondary color: #07f4f4 Vivid Cyan
  • Accent color 1: #ffffff White
  • Accent color 2: #747578 Gray

Typography

  • h1 text font: Roboto with 100 weight, color is default
  • h2, h3, h4 text fonts: Roboto with 400 weight
  • Paragraph text font: Roboto with default
  • Caption text font: Roboto with 200 weight and 0.7rem
